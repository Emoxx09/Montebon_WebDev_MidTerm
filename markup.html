Why is That Road Less Taken?

Written by Khyle Montebon



Walking along this trail

Along with all the others

I stare at the other path

A disturbing thought bothers



"Why shouldn't we follow that path?

Why continue walking here?

Surely walking along that path

Is not something to be feared!"



I let out a huge breath

And did the unthinkable

I tried out the road less taken

Then realized the incredible



Some paths are just better left untaken

It'll only be harder, you see

Just like there are mountains you shouldn't climb

Some paths lead to unreasonable guarantee
